Frequently Asked Questions

How does the affiliate commission model work? +
We earn a small commission when users purchase furniture through our platform. This helps keep our service free for most users while ensuring we show real, available products from trusted retailers.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
What's included in the 7-day trial? +
The 7-day trial includes full access to all Professional features. You can process unlimited rooms, use bulk upload, and export high-resolution images. No credit card required to start.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing? +
Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly billing. Professional plan is $11.99/month when billed annually ($143.88/year instead of $179.88).
What happens to my designs if I downgrade? +
All your previous designs remain accessible even if you downgrade. You can always view and share them, but creating new designs will be subject to your new plan's limits.
