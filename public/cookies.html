Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and improve your overall experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Dikoora uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Process payments securely

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, authentication, and accessibility.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
sb-auth-token Authentication and session management Session
sb-refresh-token Maintains login state 7 days

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your design preferences and room style choices.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
design_preferences Remembers your room type and style preferences 30 days
color_preferences Stores your preferred color schemes 30 days

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our platform. This helps us improve our services.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - identifies sessions 24 hours

Payment Cookies

These cookies are used by our payment processor (Stripe) to enable secure transactions.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
__stripe_mid Fraud prevention and detection 1 year
__stripe_sid Session identification for payments 30 minutes

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow certain third-party services to place cookies on your device:

  • Supabase: For authentication and database services
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (if enabled)

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Browse in "private" or "incognito" mode

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on Dikoora:

  • You may not be able to stay logged in
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some features may not work correctly
  • Payment processing may be affected

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
